The Engine

Four systems. One map.

01

GPS Stream Processor

Every metric calculated from the raw GPS stream. No platform-reported values. Two athletes on the same run produce identical RMM values regardless of device.

02

Route Grading System

Objective difficulty grades from GPS data alone. No manual tags. No subjective input. Upload a GPX file, get a grade. Same file, same grade. Always.

03

Runner Performance Score

A proprietary fitness score on a 90-day rolling window. Five components. Continuous decay. Your score reflects what you are, not what you were.

04

Race Readiness Engine

Are you ready for this route? Five checks against your training history. Percentage-to-threshold visibility. Honest answers, not hopeful ones.
