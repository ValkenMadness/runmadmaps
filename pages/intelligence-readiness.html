The Race Readiness Indicator

Five checks against your 90-day training history

What It Is

Five checks. One honest answer.

Pick a target route. The Race Readiness engine runs five checks against your 90-day training history. Each check shows your percentage-to-threshold — exactly how close you are and exactly what's missing.

No hopeful guesses. No "you've got this" motivation. Data. Are you ready, or aren't you?

The Five Checks

What gets measured

01

Distance Coverage

Have you run far enough in a single activity? At least one run must cover a percentage of the target route distance within your 90-day window.

02

Volume Load

Have you accumulated enough total distance? Your 90-day total must exceed the route distance multiplied by a training factor.

03

Performance Index

Have you performed well enough on comparable terrain? At least one activity on a similar or harder grade must meet the performance threshold.

04

Recency

Have you trained recently? A minimum number of activities in the last 21 days proves you're currently active, not coasting on old fitness.

05

Elevation Coverage

Have you climbed enough? Your 90-day elevation gain must cover the route's elevation demands. Auto-passes for flat routes under 50m gain.

The Verdict

Three possible answers

READY

5 of 5 pass. Go. The training supports this challenge.

CLOSE

4 of 5 pass. Almost. One specific gap to address.

NOT YET

Fewer than 4 pass. More preparation needed. Gaps identified.
