Runner Performance Score

Your running fitness. Calculated.

The Runner Performance Score (RPS) is a single, GPS-derived number that reflects how fit you are for Cape Peninsula terrain. Five components — endurance, speed efficiency, hill capacity, pace consistency, and terrain variety — calculated from every activity and weighted against the Peninsula's actual demands.

Objective Route Grading

The Route Analyzer reads your GPX file and calculates a difficulty class (A–F), a Terrain Difficulty Score (1–10), and a full breakdown of the route's demands — distance, elevation gain, elevation density, sustained climb count, and an elevation profile. No estimates. No trail ratings from unknown sources. Pure GPS.

Upload a GPX file from any device. The analyzer returns your grade card in seconds — class, TDS, effort descriptor, and a full elevation profile.

Race Readiness Check

Find out if you're ready to race.

The Race Readiness Indicator compares your current fitness profile against the demands of a specific event. Five checks — endurance reserve, hill capacity threshold, speed efficiency floor, pace consistency rating, and terrain exposure — return one of three verdicts: READY, CLOSE, or NOT YET.
