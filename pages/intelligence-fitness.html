The Fitness Indicator

Your Runner Performance Score — 90 days of truth

What It Is

A score that decays as fast as fitness does

The Runner Performance Score is a proprietary fitness score on a 90-day rolling window with continuous exponential decay. Five weighted components measured against Peninsula-calibrated benchmarks. Your score reflects what you are right now — not what you were three months ago.

Every activity you log feeds the score. Every day you don't train, the score decays. There are no shortcuts and no coasting. The number is honest.

The Engine

Five components. Weighted. Honest.

25%

Distance

Total kilometres in your 90-day window, decay-weighted. Recent runs count more than old ones.

30%

Speed Efficiency

How fast you cover ground — adjusted for terrain difficulty. A fast gorge run counts more than a fast flat run.

20%

Elevation

Total DEM-corrected elevation gain, decay-weighted. The Peninsula rewards those who climb.

15%

Pace Consistency

How even are your splits — normalised for gradient. Hills don't penalise you. Inconsistency does.

10%

Frequency

How often you train. Three runs a week beats one long weekend effort. Consistency matters.
