1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

2. How RMM Uses Cookies

RMM uses a minimal number of cookies, strictly for platform functionality and security:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Authentication Keeps you logged in during your session and across visits Session / 30 days
Security Prevents cross-site request forgery (CSRF) attacks Session
Preferences Remembers your map settings and display preferences 1 year

3. Analytics

If we implement analytics (such as Vercel Analytics), we will use privacy-respecting, cookieless analytics where possible. If any analytics tool requires cookies, we will update this policy and provide notice.

We do not use advertising cookies. We do not use tracking cookies. We do not share cookie data with third parties.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of the Platform, such as staying logged in.

5. Changes

If we add new cookie types, we will update this policy and notify you.
