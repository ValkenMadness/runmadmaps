What It Is

Upload a GPX file. Get a grade.

Every route receives a Difficulty Class (A through F), a Terrain Difficulty Score (1–10), and an Effort Descriptor. The grade comes from GPS data alone — no manual tags, no subjective input, no curator opinions. Same file, same grade. Always.

A is hardest. Intentional inversion — A-grade athletes, A-races, A-game. The grade describes the route, not the athlete.

The Grade System

Six grades. A through F.

A Extreme

Gorge-class. Relentless sustained brutality.

B Performance

Serious mountain terrain. Tests strong athletes.

C Mountain

Real climbing. Demands fitness and preparation.

D Conditioning

Moderate hills. Honest sustained effort.

E Accessible

Rolling to flat. Buildable. Entry point.

F Recovery

Flat, short. Active recovery.
